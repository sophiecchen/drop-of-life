Working for a Change

International Efforts

One of the most notable ways people are combating the clean water crisis is through international efforts. International organizations are collaborating with regional governments to come up with sustainable, long term solutions. Agencies of other countries are also stepping in to lend a hand. These efforts have transformed the clean water crisis from one country's problem to a worldwide effort.

Technologies

In order to minimize pollution and increase access, people are developing new innovations that can be used in all sorts of communities, underdeveloped or developed. These seemingly simple technologies can make a great impact, and they are developed by people who want to see change, people who have been personally affected by the clean water crisis. Read more to learn about Super Socks, Drinkable Books, and other wacky but promising innovations.

What Can You Do?

There are many things you can do right now to take action and help the clean water crisis. Some are as simple as donating or conserving water when you take a shower, brush your teeth, or wash the dishes. While it may seem that one person makes little difference, a whole community that conserves water and reduces pollution can make a real impact. Spread the word; tell your friends and your family about the clean water crisis and the importance of helping out.
